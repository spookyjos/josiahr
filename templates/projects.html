Flask x Babylon.js x Vue.js Website

A website I created to showcase my friends' newfound knowledge of Blender and Unity, as well as being the end result of following a Flask guide, The Flask Mega Tutorial by Miguel Grinberg, during an internship at A*STAR IHPC. Took ten days to plan, code and debug any errors. Faced difficulties dealing with the default camera my friends deployed being overwritten by Babylon.js, had to get help to visualise the vectors used to place the camera properly. All CSS hard-coded in, hence the simplistic and rather backward design.
Site accessible at https://wow-ihpc.herokuapp.com



Google CSSI Website

Got to experience the Googler life for two weeks at Google APAC HQ, as well as meet many coder friends that I still keep in contact with to this date.
